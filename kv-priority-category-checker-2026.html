Complete Guide

KV Admission Priority System Explained 2026

KV admission ka priority system samajhna bahut zaroori hai. Ye guide har parent ke liye useful hai.

KV Priority System Kya Hota Hai?

Kendriya Vidyalaya mein admission ek priority-based system par kaam karta hai. Jab available seats se zyada applications aate hain (jo aksar hota hai), tab KV Sangathan official priority order ke hisaab se students ko seats allot karta hai.

Priority mainly parent ke job type se determine hoti hai — Central Government, State Government, ya Private. Iske baad social category aur special quotas bhi role play karte hain.

KV Sangathan har saal admission guidelines update karta hai. Latest aur exact guidelines ke liye kvsangathan.nic.in zaroor check karein.

Priority 1 Kiski Hoti Hai?

Priority 1 — sabse highest priority — unhe milti hai jo KV Sangathan ke direct employees hain ya Central Government ke Defence (Army, Navy, Air Force) serving employees hain. Ye log transferable hote hain aur KV schools ki original foundation hi unke families ke liye thi.

  • KV / Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan employees
  • Defence Ministry employees (serving)
  • Central Government transferable employees
  • Ministry of Education employees

Single Girl Child Ka Special Benefit

KV admission mein Single Girl Child ko special consideration milti hai. Agar kisi family mein sirf ek beti hai (koi bhai nahi), toh woh Single Girl Child quota ke tahat additional priority pa sakti hai. Ye provision government ki "Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao" scheme se aligned hai.

Important: Single Girl Child benefit sirf tabhi milta hai jab parent official affidavit submit kare ki ye unki ek aur only beti hai. Supporting documents submit karne hote hain.

EWS aur BPL Ka Kya Role Hai?

Right to Education (RTE) Act ke tahat, EWS (Economically Weaker Section) aur BPL (Below Poverty Line) category ke students ke liye 25% seats reserved hain — aur ye seats priority system se alag hain. Iska matlab hai ki even if parent private job mein hai, EWS/BPL certificate hone par admission ki chances bahut improve ho jaati hain.

  • EWS certificate required: Annual family income ₹8 lakh se kam honi chahiye
  • BPL certificate state government dwara issued hona chahiye
  • 25% RTE seats parent ke job type se independent hain
  • Ye seats regular priority seats se alag count hoti hain

Private Job Walon Ka Admission Hota Hai?

Haan hota hai — lekin sirf tab jab higher priority categories ke baad seats vacant rahe. Private job employees Priority 5 (lowest) pe aate hain. Agar ek KV mein 40 seats hain aur Priority 1-4 se hi 40 applications aa gaye, toh private job walon ko seat milna mushkil ho sakti hai.

Tip for Private Employees: Agar aap EWS ya SC/ST category mein aate hain, toh reservation benefit milega. Also, Single Girl Child quota se additional weightage milti hai. Inhe zaroor use karein.

Admission Chances Kaise Improve Karein?

  • Early registration karein — notification aate hi form bharen
  • Agar EWS/BPL eligible hain toh certificate banwa lein pehle se
  • Single Girl Child affidavit ready rakhein
  • Multiple KV schools mein apply karein (closest KV prefer karein)
  • Sahi documents ready rakhein — birth certificate, address proof, category certificate
  • Previous KV school ka TC agar available ho toh advantage hota hai

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

KV admission priority ke baare mein sabse common sawaal aur unke detailed jawaab.

KV priority category kya hoti hai?+
KV priority category ek system hai jisme parent ke job type ke aadhar par admission preference decide ki jaati hai. Priority 1 (highest) Defence/CG employees ke liye hoti hai, aur Priority 5 (lowest) private employees ke liye. Jab seats limited hoon, priority wale pehle seat paate hain.
KV admission mein sabse high priority kiski hoti hai?+
Priority 1 — highest — KV Sangathan ke apne employees aur Central Government Defence (serving) employees ke bache ko milti hai. Iske baad CG transferable employees aate hain. Ye isliye hai kyunki KV schools originally military aur government families ke liye hi banaye gaye the.
Single Girl Child ka KV admission mein kya benefit hai?+
Single Girl Child ko additional priority weightage milti hai — Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao scheme ke tahat. Iske liye parent ko affidavit submit karna hota hai ki unki sirf ek beti hai. Ye benefit priority levels ke andar apply hoti hai aur selection chances improve karta hai.
EWS / BPL category ka KV admission mein kya role hai?+
RTE (Right to Education) Act ke tahat 25% seats EWS aur BPL category ke liye reserved hain. Ye seats priority system se alag hain — matlab even private job wale ya low priority wale EWS certificate par admission pa sakte hain. EWS ke liye annual family income ₹8 lakh se kam honi chahiye.
Private job walon ka KV mein admission hota hai kya?+
Haan, hota hai — lekin sirf jab seats vacant rahe Priority 1–4 se. Private employees Priority 5 mein aate hain. Urban areas mein jahan KV mein demand zyada hoti hai, wahan chances kam hote hain. Lekin agar EWS certificate hai ya Single Girl Child hai toh chances better hote hain.
SC/ST category ke liye KV mein kya reservation hai?+
SC/ST category ke students ke liye RTE provisions ke tahat reservation milti hai. Additionally, general admission seats mein bhi SC/ST ko preference milti hai. SC/ST parents agar Central Government employee hain toh combined benefit milta hai — yani priority aur category dono ka advantage.
KV admission mein transferable job ka kya faayda hai?+
Transferable job walon ko higher priority milti hai kyunki KV schools originally un families ke liye banaye gaye the jinke parents frequently transfer hote hain. Central Government transferable employees Priority 1 mein aate hain jabki non-transferable Priority 2 mein. Isliye job letter mein "transferable" clearly mention hona chahiye.
Kya OBC category ka KV admission mein koi special benefit hai?+
OBC category ke students ke liye KV Class 1 mein 27% reservation hoti hai under OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) category. Ye benefit tabhi milega jab valid OBC certificate available ho aur NCL (Non-Creamy Layer) status prove ho sake. OBC certificate state government dwara issued hona chahiye.
Kya Defence retired (Ex-Serviceman) ke bache ko priority milti hai?+
Haan, Ex-Servicemen (retired Defence personnel) ke bache ko Priority 2 milti hai — jo serving employees ke baad aate hain lekin State Government se pehle. Iske liye valid Ex-Serviceman certificate aur discharge certificate submit karna hota hai.
Kya ye KV Priority Checker official tool hai?+
Nahi, ye RajDailyTools ka educational tool hai jo official KV Sangathan guidelines par based hai. Ye KV Sangathan ya kisi government agency se affiliated nahi hai. Tool ke results informational hain — final confirmation ke liye hamesha official KV school ya kvsangathan.nic.in se verify karein.
