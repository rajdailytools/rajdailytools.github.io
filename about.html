Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is RajDailyTools free to use?+
Yes, absolutely! Every single tool on RajDailyTools is 100% free. No subscriptions, no premium tiers, no hidden fees — now, and forever. Our mission is to make powerful tools accessible to everyone, especially students and people who cannot afford expensive software.
Do I need to create an account to use the tools?+
No login or registration is required to use any tool on our platform. Just open the tool page and start using it immediately. We respect your time and privacy — no forced sign-ups, no email harvesting, ever.
Are the calculations and results accurate?+
Yes. Every tool is built by Raj Bhai — a BSc Physics graduate — with thorough formula verification and testing. We follow standard industry formulas and conventions. For critical financial or medical decisions, we recommend cross-verifying with a certified professional.
Can I suggest a new tool to be built?+
Absolutely! Tool suggestions from users are what shape our roadmap. Email your idea to contact@rajdailytools.com or use the Contact page. If your suggestion solves a real problem for many users, we'll build it and credit you for the idea.
Is my data safe when I use these tools?+
Yes. Most tools run entirely in your browser — your data never leaves your device. We do not collect, store, or sell personal data. Your privacy is a core value, not an afterthought. We are also fully AdSense compliant and follow Google's publisher policies.
