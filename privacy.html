Section 02
Information We Collect

We collect two types of information depending on how you use our platform. Most tools on RajDailyTools run entirely in your browser and collect no personal data whatsoever.

Personal Information (only when you contact us):

  • Your name (if provided in the contact form)
  • Your email address (for responding to your queries)
  • Your message content (to address your request or feedback)

Non-Personal / Automatically Collected Information:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Pages visited, time spent, and navigation patterns
  • Referring website or search engine
  • Session duration and interaction data
ℹ️
Important: Most of our tools run entirely client-side in your browser. When you use a calculator or converter, the data you enter never leaves your device. It is processed locally and discarded when you close the page.

Section 04
Cookies Policy

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use them. RajDailyTools uses cookies in a limited, responsible way.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Analytical Cookies: Used by Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site, which pages they visit, and how long they stay. This data is aggregated and anonymous.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to serve relevant advertisements. These cookies track browsing activity to show more relevant ads (see Section 05 for details).
  • Functional Cookies: Small cookies that help the website function correctly, remember your preferences, and improve performance during your session.
💡
Your cookie control: You can manage, disable, or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features on RajDailyTools.
📊 Analytics 📢 Advertising ⚙️ Functional 🔒 No tracking profiles
📢
Section 05
Google AdSense & Advertising

RajDailyTools uses Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. AdSense helps us keep all tools free by generating revenue through relevant ads shown to visitors.

How Google AdSense works on our site:

  • Google uses the DoubleClick cookie to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website and other sites on the internet
  • Google AdSense uses interest-based advertising (also known as personalised advertising) to show you ads relevant to your interests
  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to RajDailyTools and other websites
  • Users may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings page
  • Alternatively, users can opt out of third-party vendor cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page
🔗
Learn more: Google's use of advertising cookies is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy, which you can review at policies.google.com/privacy. RajDailyTools has no control over Google's cookie practices.
🔗
Section 06
Third-Party Services

RajDailyTools integrates with a small number of trusted third-party services to help operate and improve the platform. Each of these services has its own privacy policy and data practices.

  • Google Analytics: Tracks anonymised visitor behaviour, page views, session duration, and geographic data to help us understand how the platform is used and improve it
  • Google AdSense: Displays relevant advertisements to support the free operation of RajDailyTools (see Section 05 for details)
  • Google Fonts: Loads typography files from Google's CDN — this may involve your IP address being sent to Google servers but no personal data is stored
  • Embedded Content: Some tools may embed content from other platforms (such as YouTube videos or Google Forms for contact). These embeds are subject to the third party's own privacy policy
⚠️
External links: Our website may contain links to third-party websites. Once you click on an external link and leave RajDailyTools, we have no control over that site's content or privacy practices. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

Section 07
Data Protection & Security

We take the security of your information seriously. RajDailyTools implements multiple layers of protection to ensure your data remains safe and confidential at all times.

  • Our website uses HTTPS encryption (SSL/TLS) to ensure all data transmitted between your browser and our server is encrypted and secure
  • Most tools run entirely in your browser — any data you enter into a calculator, converter, or text tool is never sent to our servers
  • We do not store any calculator inputs, query data, or tool-specific information on our servers
  • Access to any contact form data is restricted to authorised personnel only (Raj Bhai and trusted team members)
  • We regularly review and update our security practices to address emerging threats
  • We retain contact form submissions only as long as necessary to respond to your query
✅
Our security commitment: While no system is 100% immune to security risks, we follow industry best practices and continuously work to protect your data. If you ever suspect a security issue, please contact us immediately at rajdailytools@gmail.com.
✅
