Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is RajDailyTools completely free to use?+
Yes, absolutely! Every tool on RajDailyTools is 100% free. No subscriptions, no premium tiers, no hidden fees. We believe powerful tools should be accessible to everyone, especially students and people who can't afford expensive software.
Do I need to create an account or login?+
No login or registration is required to use any tool. Just visit the tool page and start using it instantly. We respect your time and privacy — no forced sign-ups ever.
Are the calculations accurate and reliable?+
Yes. Each tool is built by Raj Bhai — a BSc Physics graduate — with thorough testing and validation. We follow standard formulas and industry conventions. For critical financial decisions, we recommend cross-verifying with a certified professional.
Does RajDailyTools work on mobile phones?+
Yes! Every tool is designed mobile-first. The entire platform is fully responsive and optimized for smartphones, tablets, and desktops. No app download needed — just open your browser and use.
How many tools does RajDailyTools currently have?+
We are actively building and launching new tools every week. Our founder's vision is to reach 30,000+ tools across all categories. Check our Latest Tools section to see what's newly launched.
Can I suggest a tool that should be built?+
Absolutely! We love user suggestions. Email us at contact@rajdailytools.com with your tool idea. If it solves a real problem for many people, we'll build it and credit you for the suggestion.
Is my data safe when I use these tools?+
Yes. Most of our tools run entirely in your browser — your data never leaves your device. We don't collect, store, or sell personal data. Your privacy is our priority.
