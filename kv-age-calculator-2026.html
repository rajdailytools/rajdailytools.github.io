Complete Guide

KV Admission 2026–27: Complete Guide

Everything you need to know about Kendriya Vidyalaya admission, age rules, and required documents.

KV Admission 2026–27 Kab Shuru Hogi?

Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) har saal February–March mein admission notification release karta hai. Session 2026–27 ke liye notification jald hi official website par release hone ki umeed hai. Parents ko advise kiya jaata hai ki regularly official website check karte rahen.

Official notification ke liye visit karein: kvsangathan.nic.in — sab se latest aur accurate information wahan hi milegi.

Class 1 Admission: Online Process

Class 1 ke liye admission process mainly online hoti hai through KVS official portal. Lottery/draw system ke through seats allot ki jaati hain jab seats se zyada applications aate hain.

  • Online registration KVS official portal par karna hota hai
  • Documents verification school mein offline hoti hai
  • Excess applications par lucky draw/lottery hoti hai
  • Provisional admission list online publish hoti hai
  • Single girl child ko special priority milti hai

Balvatika Admission Process

Balvatika — KV ka pre-school program — 3 levels mein hota hai (BV1, BV2, BV3). Ye programme 3 se 6 saal ke bachcho ke liye hai aur play-based learning par focused hai.

Class 2 to 12: Offline Admissions

Class 2 se Class 12 tak admissions offline hoti hain aur sirf tabhi jab school mein vacant seats hoti hain. Directly school se contact karna padta hai. Koi centralized online system nahi hai in classes ke liye.

Required Documents for KV Admission

  • Birth Certificate: Municipal Corporation, Gram Panchayat ya Hospital issued
  • Address Proof: Aadhaar card, Voter ID, Passport, ya Utility bill
  • Category Certificate: SC/ST/OBC/EWS certificate (if applicable)
  • Service Certificate: Parent ka service certificate (if Central Govt/Defence employee)
  • Transfer Certificate (TC): Class 2 aur above ke liye previous school se TC
  • Passport Size Photos: 4–6 latest passport size photos of the child
  • Aadhaar Card: Child ka Aadhaar (if available)
  • Income Certificate: EWS category ke liye

KV Admission Priority Order

  • Priority 1: KV employees ke bache
  • Priority 2: Central Government transferable employees ke bache
  • Priority 3: Central Autonomous Bodies employees
  • Priority 4: State Government transferable employees
  • Priority 5: Single Girl Child (special consideration)
  • Priority 6: SC/ST students under RTE provisions
  • Priority 7: General public (remaining seats par)
Important: Yahan di gayi information official KV guidelines par based hai. Final verification ke liye please kvsangathan.nic.in zaroor check karein. Rules har saal thodi bahut change ho sakti hain.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

KV admission age ke baare mein sabse common sawaal aur unke detailed jawaab.

KV Class 1 mein admission ke liye age limit kya hai?+
KV Class 1 admission ke liye minimum age 6 years aur maximum age 8 years honi chahiye — dono 1st April 2026 ko calculated hoti hain. SC/ST/PH students ke liye maximum age mein 2 saal ki relaxation milti hai.
KV admission ke liye age kaise calculate karein?+
KV mein age hamesha 1st April of the academic year par calculate ki jaati hai. Session 2026–27 ke liye reference date 1 April 2026 hai. Hamare tool mein sirf DOB enter karein — baaki calculation automatic hogi.
Kya age 1 April ko hi calculate hoti hai ya admission date par?+
Haan, age hamesha 1st April of the academic session par calculate hoti hai — admission date par nahi. Chahe admission March mein ho ya June mein, reference date 1 April hi rehti hai. Ye KV Sangathan ka official rule hai jo har session par apply hota hai.
Agar mera bachcha thodi si age se miss kar raha hai toh kya karein?+
Age miss hone par generally koi relaxation nahi milti (SC/ST/PH ko 2 saal milti hai). Aapko next session ka wait karna hoga. Hamare tool mein "Next Year Eligibility" prediction feature hai jo batata hai ki aapka bachcha kaunse session mein eligible hoga.
KV Class 1 admission online hota hai ya offline?+
Class 1 ke liye admission online registration ke zariye hota hai — KVS official portal par. Registration ke baad documents verification ke liye school jaana padta hai. Agar applications zyada hone par lottery draw hota hai. Class 2 aur above ke liye admissions offline hain.
Balvatika kya hota hai aur kiske liye hai?+
Balvatika KV Sangathan ka Pre-School program hai jo 3 levels mein hota hai: BV1 (3-4 years), BV2 (4-5 years), aur BV3 (5-6 years). Ye play-based learning programme neighbourhood children ke liye bhi available hai, sirf KV employees ke liye nahi.
SC/ST/PH category ke students ke liye age relaxation milti hai?+
Haan, SC/ST aur PH (Physically Handicapped) category ke students ke liye maximum age mein generally 2 saal ki relaxation milti hai. Minimum age mein koi relaxation nahi hoti. Exact rules ke liye latest KV official notification zaroor check karein.
KV admission ke liye kaunse documents chahiye?+
Main documents: Birth Certificate (compulsory), Address Proof (Aadhaar/Voter ID), Category Certificate (if applicable), Service Certificate (for govt employees), Passport Size Photos, aur Class 2+ ke liye Transfer Certificate (TC). Exact list ke liye school se check karein.
KV admission notification 2026–27 kab release hogi?+
KV Admission 2026–27 notification February–March 2026 mein release hone ki umeed hai — ye har saal ka pattern hai. Official confirmation ke liye kvsangathan.nic.in regularly check karte rahen.
Kya ye KV Age Calculator official tool hai?+
Nahi, ye tool RajDailyTools ka ek free educational tool hai jo official KV Sangathan guidelines par based hai. Ye KV Sangathan ya kisi government agency se affiliated nahi hai. Results informational purposes ke liye hain — final confirmation hamesha official website ya school se karein.
